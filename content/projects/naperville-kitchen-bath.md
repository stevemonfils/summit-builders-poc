The homeowners wanted to modernize their kitchen and master bath without changing the home's classic colonial character.

## Project Details

- **Kitchen:** 280 sq ft — Custom shaker cabinetry, quartz waterfall island, Wolf range, Sub-Zero fridge
- **Master Bath:** 120 sq ft — Walk-in shower with linear drain, freestanding tub, heated porcelain tile
- **Timeline:** 10 weeks
- **Special Features:** Under-cabinet LED lighting with dimmer zones, soft-close everything, ceiling-mounted rain shower

We worked with the clients' interior designer to ensure finishes, fixtures, and hardware all tied together cohesively.
