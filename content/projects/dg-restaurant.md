A complete transformation of a vacant retail space into a full-service restaurant. The project required extensive mechanical, electrical, and plumbing work to support the commercial kitchen.

## Project Details

- **Size:** 4,200 sq ft
- **Timeline:** 6 months
- **Scope:** Full gut renovation, commercial kitchen, custom millwork bar, outdoor patio enclosure
- **Challenges:** Working within historic district guidelines while meeting modern commercial codes

We coordinated with the village, health department, and liquor commission to ensure all permits and inspections were handled smoothly.
