This custom-built modern farmhouse brings together rustic warmth and contemporary design. The clients wanted an open floor plan that worked for both everyday family life and large gatherings.

## Project Details

- **Size:** 3,800 sq ft
- **Bedrooms:** 4
- **Bathrooms:** 3.5
- **Timeline:** 14 months
- **Features:** Custom white oak cabinetry, heated tile floors, 12-foot ceilings in great room, integrated smart home system
